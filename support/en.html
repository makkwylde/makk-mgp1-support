About the App

MGP-1 Makk Guitar Processor is a high-quality guitar processor compatible with Audio Unit V3. It features 10 professional-grade effects and a tuner, supporting music production in DAWs.

Key Features

  • Tuner (high-speed, high-precision pitch detection)
  • 10 professional-grade effects
  • Real-time processing (low latency)
  • Universal app (iPhone, iPad, Mac support)
  • Complete offline functionality

System Requirements

  • iOS 18.5 or later
  • macOS 14.0 or later
  • Audio Unit V3 compatible DAW
