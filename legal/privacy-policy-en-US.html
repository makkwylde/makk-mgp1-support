1. Overview

MGP-1 Makk Guitar Processor (the "App") respects your privacy and collects only minimal information.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the following information:

  • Subscription Information: Purchase status and expiration date via App Store Connect API

3. Information We Don't Collect

The App does NOT collect the following information:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Audio data or recordings
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Device information
  • Usage data
  • Crash reports

4. Offline Operation

The App operates completely offline:

  • No audio data is transmitted
  • Works without internet connection
  • Presets are saved locally
  • Full features available offline if subscription is active

5. Subscription Verification

Subscription verification works as follows:

  • Online: Real-time verification via App Store Connect API
  • Offline: Verification using locally cached expiration date
  • Expired: Automatically reverts to free version limitations

6. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Preset settings
  • Subscription expiration date (cached)
  • User settings

7. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share collected information with third parties. Subscription information is processed only through App Store Connect.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through App Store reviews.
